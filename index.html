What We Do

Four Pillars of Community Strength

Every program we run is built around the lived experiences of Somali American families — designed with community, not just for community.

01
🩺
Health

Bridging Somali families to accessible, culturally informed healthcare navigation and wellness resources across the Twin Cities.

02
📚
Education

Mentorship and academic support programs helping youth navigate their dual identity and achieve long-term educational success.

03
💼
Financial Stability

Workforce development, financial literacy, and economic empowerment initiatives that build generational wealth for families.

04
🤝
Community Safety Net

Emergency support, community advocacy, and safety resources that ensure no Somali family in Minnesota faces hardship alone.

Our Mission

A 501(c)(3) rooted in dignity, culture, and belonging

Founded in December 2024, Somali American Partnerships was built to fill a critical gap — community-centered programs that honor Somali culture while building lasting bridges in Minnesota.

Minneapolis, Minnesota  ·  Est. 2024  ·  IRS 501(c)(3) registered

"We build the kind of organization we wished had existed when our families first arrived — a trusted partner at every stage of the journey."
